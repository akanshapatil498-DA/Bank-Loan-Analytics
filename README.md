🏦 Bank Loan Performance & Risk Analytics
📌 Business Problem

The bank wanted to analyze its loan portfolio to understand:

Loan disbursement trends over time

Borrower risk profiles

Repayment behavior

Impact of verification status

Regional loan distribution

The objective was to identify risk patterns and improve credit assessment & recovery strategies.

🛠️ Tools Used

Excel – Initial exploration

MySQL – Loan performance analysis

Power BI – Interactive Dashboard

📊 Project Workflow
1️⃣ Data Preparation

Cleaned CSV datasets

Removed duplicates

Standardized loan amount & date formats

Handled missing values

2️⃣ KPI Analysis (SQL)
✔ Year-wise Loan Disbursement

2011 had highest loan disbursement

Rapid growth observed between 2009–2011

✔ Grade & Sub-grade Revolving Balance

B4 & B5 sub-grades had highest balance

Lower grades showed higher risk

✔ Verified vs Non-Verified Payments

Verified borrowers contributed higher repayment

Indicates stronger creditworthiness

✔ State-wise Loan Distribution

California had highest loan count

Seasonal patterns observed in monthly data

✔ Home Ownership vs Repayment

Mortgage holders showed better repayment trends

Renters showed lower repayment reliability

📈 Key Insights

Verified borrowers are less risky

Certain grades show high default probability

Mortgage holders are more stable borrowers

Specific states generate high loan volume

💡 Business Recommendations

Strengthen verification process

Focus on low-risk borrower segments

Monitor high-risk grades carefully

Improve risk-based pricing strategy
